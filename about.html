About Us

WHO ARE WE?

We are a small team placed in the UK.

WHAT WE DEVELOP?

We are developing a web/mobile emergency alert app for use in the UK. BEAS(Britain Emergency Alert System) provides users with a centralised source of information regarding any potential emergencies in their area,
whether they are health, natural or safety related. The information comes from both official sources and through verifying user reports, which can be submitted through the app. The app also contains communication
features that allow users to chat with their contacts through the app.

WHY WE DEVELOP THIS APP?

As of right now, there is no other app that covers a wide array of emergencies at once while allowing users to file reports of their own. We began developing BEAS as a way of filling this gap and providing a useful resource to the public.
